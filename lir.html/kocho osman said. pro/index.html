Who We Are

Founded in Lira, Uganda, LYI is a grassroots youth initiative that believes every young person deserves access to quality education, mentorship, and vocational opportunities.

We work alongside communities, local partners, and dedicated volunteers to create lasting change — one young person at a time.

Our Mission

"To empower youth in Lira through education, skills training, and leadership development."

About LYI

Built on Strong Foundations

Everything we do is guided by core values that put youth first and communities at the center.

What We Do

Our Programs

From classroom learning to hands-on vocational training, our programs are designed to equip youth with the tools they need to thrive.

Academic Support & Tutoring

Supplementary classes and mentorship for primary and secondary school students, focusing on literacy, numeracy, and science.

Digital Literacy & ICT Training

Hands-on computer skills training, internet literacy, and introduction to digital tools for employability and entrepreneurship.

Vocational Skills Training

Practical courses in tailoring, carpentry, agriculture, and other trades that open doors to meaningful livelihoods.

Leadership & Mentorship

Structured mentorship matching youth with experienced professionals, alongside leadership workshops and civic education.

Entrepreneurship Bootcamps

Intensive workshops covering business planning, financial literacy, and startup basics — helping youth turn ideas into income.

Community Outreach

Awareness campaigns, school visits, and community events that promote the value of education and youth empowerment.
